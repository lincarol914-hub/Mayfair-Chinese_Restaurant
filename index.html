现点现做 · 火候见真章Cooked to Order

美味庄园坐落于朴次茅斯市政广场旁，以传统川菜与东北风味为本，所有菜品现点现做，从香酥鸭到麻辣烤鲈鱼，皆讲究锅气与火候。我们亦提供火锅与烤肉自助，邀您与亲友围炉共聚。 Set beside Portsmouth's Guildhall Square, Mayfair celebrates traditional Sichuan and Northeastern cooking. Every dish is prepared to order — from crispy aromatic duck to whole grilled seabass — with all the heat and aroma of the wok. Gather friends around our hot pot & barbecue buffet, too.
