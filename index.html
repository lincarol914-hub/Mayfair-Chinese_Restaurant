我们是一家深受当地食客喜爱的中餐馆，菜品丰富，涵盖头盘小食、汤品、凉菜、肉类热菜、时蔬、烤鱼、干锅系列，以及火锅 + 烧烤自助。无论是家庭聚餐还是朋友聚会，都能在这里找到心仪的美味。 A neighbourhood favourite with a wide-ranging menu — starters, soups, cold plates, meat dishes, vegetables, grilled fish and dry pots, plus an all-you-can-eat hot pot & barbecue. Whether a family dinner or a night with friends, there's something to love.
