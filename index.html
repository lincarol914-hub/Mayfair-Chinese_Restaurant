Our Philosophy

品牌理念The Mayfair Way

美味庄园融汇川粤之精髓，承袭传统烹饪智慧，以至臻食材、精湛技艺与雅致环境，呈现当代中式餐饮的至高境界。 Mayfair brings together the soul of Sichuan and Cantonese cooking — fine ingredients, masterful technique and a refined setting — for a contemporary expression of Chinese dining at its finest.

每一道菜，皆是文化与匠心的交融；每一次相聚，皆是尊贵而雅致的礼遇。 Every dish is a meeting of culture and craft; every gathering, an occasion to be savoured.
